Cannon Balls 3D

Get ready to aim, shoot, and destroy in this addictive physics-based cannon game!

Game Overview

Cannon Balls 3D is an exciting physics-based shooting game where your goal is to aim and fire cannon balls to destroy various structures and targets. Test your precision, timing, and strategic thinking as you progress through increasingly challenging levels.

How to Play

  • Aim your cannon by dragging to adjust direction and power
  • Release to fire cannon balls at the targets
  • Destroy all targets to complete each level
  • Use the minimum number of shots to achieve higher scores
  • Unlock new levels and challenges as you progress

Game Features

  • Realistic physics simulation
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Stunning 3D graphics
  • Intuitive controls
  • Various targets and obstacles
  • Progressive difficulty

Why Play Cannon Balls 3D?

Satisfying Physics

Experience realistic and satisfying physics as structures crumble and targets explode. Every shot feels impactful and rewarding.

Strategic Gameplay

It's not just about shooting—finding the perfect angle and power to maximize destruction requires strategy and planning.

Quick Sessions

Perfect for short gaming breaks, each level can be completed in minutes. Jump in for a quick round anytime.

Pro Tips and Strategies

Target Weak Points

Look for structural weak points in the arrangements. Hitting these points can cause chain reactions, maximizing destruction with fewer shots.

Adjust Power Carefully

The power of your shot is just as important as the angle. Sometimes a gentle tap will be more effective than full power, especially when aiming for precision hits.

Study the Physics

Take a moment to observe how different structures react to your shots. Learning the game's physics will help you predict how objects will fall and collide.

Plan Your Sequence

Sometimes the order of your shots matters. Plan which targets to hit first to create the most effective chain of destruction.
